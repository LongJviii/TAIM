
# TAIM: Compute as Currency

A decentralized compute currency protocol. A post-bubble solution for the AI monetization crisis via tokenized compute as AI-native online payment.

## What is TAIM?

TAIM creates a native currency for the AI era's core factor of production: computing power. It mints idle, soon-to-be-discarded AI Tokens into a circulating currency through over-collateralized smart contracts.

## Why TAIM?

The AI industry burns over $700 billion annually on computing infrastructure but struggles to monetize. TAIM is the **online payment protocol for the AI era**—what PayPal was to the Internet bubble, TAIM is to the AI bubble.


## How It Works

- **Minters** stake idle compute Tokens → mint TAIM
- **Users** buy TAIM → redeem real AI computing at lower cost
- **Speculators** trade TAIM → provide liquidity and price discovery

A dual-engine system: physical computing scarcity caps the ceiling; real user demand floors the bottom. Self-balancing, nearly inexhaustible liquidity.
